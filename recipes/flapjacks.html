Flapjacks

Description

Soft, chewy flapjacks. Simple yet effective, these will be gone in hours!

Ingredients

  • 250g oats
  • 125g butter
  • 125g light brown sugar
  • 3tbsp golden syrup

Steps

  1. Melt butter and sugar in saucepan
  2. Add in syrup
  3. Pour in and mix oats
  4. Put mixture in a square tin
  5. Cook until golden brown for approx. 15 minutes at 180 fan.
